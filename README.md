# Dynamics 365 Business Central Functional Portfolio

Welcome to my professional functional portfolio. This repository serves as a technical showcase of my ability to design, configure, and implement **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** solutions that solve real-world business challenges.

---

## 🚀 Core Expertise
I specialize in transforming manual business processes into automated, scalable ERP architectures, focusing on:
* **Financial Governance:** G/L Setup, Posting Groups, and Auditability.
* **Supply Chain Management:** Inventory optimization, Variant tracking, and Bin Mandatory logic.
* **Trade Cycles:** End-to-End P2P (Procure-to-Pay) and O2C (Order-to-Cash) workflows.

## 📁 Featured Projects
